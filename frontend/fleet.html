SkyLink A1

Urban commuter designed for efficiency and agility in dense city environments

Urban Air Mobility Redefined

The SkyLink A1 is our flagship urban commuter model, designed specifically for short to medium-range trips within metropolitan areas. With its compact footprint and advanced tilt-wing technology, the A1 can operate from rooftop vertiports with minimal space requirements.

2 Passengers
150 km Range
180 km/h Cruise Speed
25 min Charge Time

Key Features

  • Tilt-wing design for efficient forward flight
  • Advanced noise reduction technology
  • Automated flight systems with manual override
  • Rapid charging capability
  • All-weather operation certification
  • Integrated safety and collision avoidance

Technical Specifications

Propulsion System 8 electric motors with distributed propulsion

Noise Level 65 dB at 100m (takeoff)
Certification EASA SC-VTOL, FAA Part 135
